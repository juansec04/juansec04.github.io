Crap Principles Evaluations

Apple

  • Contrast: Excellent - bold headings, and clear visual hierarchy make the content easy to read.
  • Repetition: Very consistent use of typgraphy, color, and layout styles across the site.
  • Alignment: Strong grid and alignment patterns make the layout visually cleand and easy to read.
  • Proximity: Content groups are spaced and separated logically for easy scanning.

Huawei

  • Contrast: Weak emphasis, too many items compete for attention.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent, feeling like some sections look like marketing blocks while others like a report, so the page feels stiched together instead of consistent.
  • Alignment: Not terrible, but visually there are many stacked menus and sections and the show more section make the layout feel heavy rather than clean.
  • Proximity: Crowded and overwhelming too, many links and categories are packed in the top area, and long blocks of text make it harder to quickly find what you need.
